Eating Disorder

Overview:
Eating disorders include conditions such as anorexia nervosa, bulimia nervosa, and binge-eating disorder. They involve unhealthy eating behaviors and preoccupation with weight or shape.

Common signs:
- Restrictive eating, bingeing, or compensatory behaviors (purging)
- Extreme preoccupation with body weight and image
- Physical complications from poor nutrition

Support & treatment:
Multi-disciplinary care with therapy (CBT, family-based), nutritional support, and medical monitoring is recommended.
