Social Anxiety Disorder

Overview:
Social anxiety disorder involves intense fear or anxiety about social situations where one may be judged, leading to avoidance or distress.

Common signs:
- Fear of scrutiny, avoidance of social interactions
- Physical symptoms like blushing, trembling, or nausea in social settings

Support & treatment:
CBT with exposure to social situations, social skills training, and medication may help reduce symptoms and improve confidence.
