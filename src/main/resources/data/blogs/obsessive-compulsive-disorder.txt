Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)

Overview:
OCD is characterized by recurrent, unwanted thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors or mental acts (compulsions) performed to reduce anxiety.

Common symptoms:
- Persistent intrusive thoughts or images
- Ritualized behaviors (washing, checking) performed to relieve distress
- Time-consuming routines that interfere with life

Support & treatment:
Exposure and response prevention (ERP), CBT, and medication (SSRIs) are effective treatments. Support and structured therapy are recommended.
