Bipolar Disorder

Overview:
Bipolar disorder is characterized by alternating mood episodes of mania/hypomania and depression. Mood swings can impact energy, activity levels, judgment, and sleep.

Common signs:
- Elevated or irritable mood, increased activity during manic phases
- Depressive episodes with low mood and reduced motivation
- Changes in sleep, appetite, and decision-making

Support & treatment:
Mood stabilizers, psychotherapy, psychoeducation, and lifestyle routines help many people manage symptoms. Regular follow-up with a clinician is important.
