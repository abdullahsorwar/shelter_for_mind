Overview:
ADHD is a neurodevelopmental condition marked by patterns of inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity that interfere with functioning or development.

Common signs:
- Difficulty sustaining attention, disorganization
- Excessive activity or fidgeting, impulsive actions

Support & treatment:
Behavioral strategies, organizational supports, psychoeducation, and medication when appropriate can reduce symptoms and improve daily functioning.
