Depression

Overview:
Depression is a common mood disorder characterized by persistent sadness, loss of interest in activities, and reduced energy. It can affect thinking, sleep, appetite, and daily functioning.

Common symptoms:
- Low mood, tearfulness, hopelessness
- Loss of interest or pleasure in activities
- Changes in appetite and sleep
- Difficulty concentrating and low energy

Support & treatment:
Treatment can include therapy (CBT, interpersonal), medication, lifestyle changes, and social support. If you are in crisis, seek immediate help.
