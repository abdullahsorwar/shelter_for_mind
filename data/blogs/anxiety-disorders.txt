Anxiety Disorders

Overview:
Anxiety disorders involve excessive worry or fear that is difficult to control and that interferes with daily life. There are several types, including generalized anxiety disorder, panic disorder, and specific phobias.

Common symptoms:
- Restlessness, tension, or feeling on edge
- Rapid heartbeat, shortness of breath, trembling
- Excessive worry, difficulty concentrating

Support & treatment:
Common approaches include cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), exposure therapy, medication, relaxation techniques, and lifestyle modifications.
