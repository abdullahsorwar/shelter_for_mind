Schizophrenia

Overview:
Schizophrenia is a severe mental health condition that can affect thinking, perceptions, emotions, and behavior. Symptoms often include hallucinations, delusions, disorganized speech, and social withdrawal.

Common symptoms:
- Auditory or visual hallucinations
- Strong beliefs not based in reality (delusions)
- Disorganized thinking, reduced emotional expression

Support & treatment:
Antipsychotic medication, psychosocial support, rehabilitation, and family education are central components of care. Early intervention improves outcomes.
