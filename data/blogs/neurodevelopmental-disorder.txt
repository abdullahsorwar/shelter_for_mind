Neurodevelopmental Disorders

Overview:
Neurodevelopmental disorders (e.g., autism spectrum disorder, intellectual disabilities) affect development of cognition, communication, or behavior and typically appear early in life.

Common features:
- Delayed or atypical development in communication, social interaction, or learning
- Variable support needs across the lifespan

Support & treatment:
Early intervention, individualized education plans, therapies (speech, occupational), and family support improve outcomes.
