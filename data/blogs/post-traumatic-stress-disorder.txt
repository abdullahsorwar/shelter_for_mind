Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

Overview:
PTSD can develop after exposure to a traumatic event. People with PTSD may relive the trauma, avoid reminders, and experience heightened arousal or negative mood.

Common symptoms:
- Flashbacks, intrusive memories, nightmares
- Avoidance of reminders, emotional numbness
- Hypervigilance, sleep disturbance, irritability

Support & treatment:
Evidence-based treatments include trauma-focused CBT, EMDR, medication when appropriate, and strong social support.
